# about

Why pipelines.py exists

Most Python content out there is either too basic to be useful or too advanced to get started with. pipelines.py is built to close that gap — real, practical Python, taught the way it's actually used by people who work with data every day.

I started this because the explanation I wish I'd had early on didn't exist — content that respects beginners without talking down to them, and that actually goes somewhere instead of stopping at "hello world."

The free content here — YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and this site — is the foundation. If you want the full structured version with projects, exercises, and a clear path from beginner to job-ready, that's what PyPath is being built for.
